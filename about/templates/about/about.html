About

This web portal aims at easing the process of verification when a candidate takes admission in any educational institute or switches between any working organizations. The main aim of this web portal is to reduce the hassle of carrying along the original certificates during the process of verification.

Instructions to use this web portal:

  • The candidates would first have to login into the portal using the Unique Identity Number given to them by the Authorization body
  • Once logged in, the candidate has the options to uploadtheir personal certificates and then generate the QR codefor their all certificates
  • The options of Login, Upload, QR code and Log out are present in the circular navigation window at the top-left of this page
  • Initially their is option of Login and when rotated, the navigation bar provides the user options of Upload, QR codeand Log out
  • Candidate can either scan the QR code or get it printed with the options available in the portal itself and present it to the verification body at the time of verification
  • The Qr code on scanning will give access to a folder containing the certificates of the candidates which can be further used by the joining body as per requirements
